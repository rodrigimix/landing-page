"The Real Driving Simulator."
Gran Turismo is a legendary racing game series developed by Polyphony Digital for PlayStation. It focuses on realistic driving physics, licensed cars, and detailed tracks. Since 1997, it has set the standard for racing simulations and remains one of the best-selling PlayStation franchises of all time.
Why You Should Play Gran Turismo 7
1
Realistic driving experience with lifelike physics, handling, and track conditions.
2
Massive variety with hundreds of cars, dozens of tracks, and endless customization.
3
Immersive visuals with ray tracing, DualSense feedback, and 3D audio.
4
Single-player campaign or competitive online racing.
